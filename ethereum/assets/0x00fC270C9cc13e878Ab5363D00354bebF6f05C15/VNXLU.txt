About VNX
VNX (www.vnx.io) is a Luxembourg-based digital asset issuance platform.
 VNX developed an end-to-end regulatory compliant platform to organize digital assets 
 offerings that includes the development of the design and structure of the offering,
 investor relations, issuance and after issuance support. VNX Platform provides a simple
 and user-friendly process for regular and 
professional investors to participate in the digital asset offerings alongside the lead investor.
